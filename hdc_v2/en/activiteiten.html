  • Striking, cutting and chiselling (2014) — Dialogue between the work of Herman De Cuyper and the Ghent artist Mark Cloet. CC De Ster, Torenstraat 6, Willebroek. In 2010, Mark Cloet made a lithograph for married couples on commission from the municipality of Willebroek. This grew into a dialogue around De Cuyper's basic techniques: clay, wood and stone.
